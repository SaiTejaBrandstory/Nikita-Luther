About Nikita

Nikita Luther is a professional poker player from Delhi, India, known for her exceptional skills and strategic gameplay. She gained prominence in the global poker community by becoming the first Indian woman to win a World Series of Poker (WSOP) bracelet in 2018, alongside her teammate Giuseppe Pantaleo in the $1,000 No-Limit Hold'em Tag Team event.

With a strong background in finance and marketing, Nikita initially started her journey in the corporate world but soon discovered her passion for poker. She has played at some of the biggest poker tournaments across the world, including the World Poker Tour (WPT), Asian Poker Tour (APT), and European Poker Tour (EPT). Her fearless and analytical approach to the game has earned her a reputation as one of India's top poker professionals.

Beyond her achievements on the felt, Nikita is also an advocate for women in poker and actively works to encourage more female participation in the sport. She has been a brand ambassador for leading poker platforms and has played a significant role in promoting the game in India.

With multiple deep runs in high-stakes events and a growing list of accomplishments, Nikita Luther continues to be a formidable force in the poker world.

More About Her Poker Journey

Over the years, Nikita has not only played but also contributed to the poker community through mentorship, brand endorsements, and poker-related content creation. She has been featured in major poker publications and interviews where she shares insights about her journey, strategies, and the growth of poker in India.

Some of her major career highlights include:

  • Winning the WSOP Bracelet in 2018
  • Runner-up finish at WPT500 Las Vegas

  • Serving as a poker brand ambassador

Skills & Education

Nikita Luther holds a bachelor's degree in finance and marketing from the University of Delhi. Her strong analytical skills and strategic mindset laid the foundation for her career in professional poker.

Key Skills:

  • Poker Strategy & Game Theory: Expertise in Texas Hold’em, Omaha, and tournament poker strategies.
  • Bankroll Management: Skilled in risk assessment and financial discipline, essential for long-term success in poker.
  • Psychological Resilience: Ability to maintain focus, read opponents, and make calculated decisions under pressure.
  • Mathematical & Probability Analysis: Strong understanding of probabilities, expected value (EV), and statistical modeling in poker.
  • Leadership & Mentorship: Actively contributes to the poker community, mentoring aspiring players and promoting the game in India.

Her dedication to mastering poker led her to become India’s first female WSOP bracelet winner, a prestigious achievement in the world of professional poker.
