About Me

Get to know more about my background, passion for poker, and professional journey.

Professional Poker Player & WSOP Bracelet Winner

Nikita Luther is a professional poker player from Delhi, India, known for her exceptional skills and strategic gameplay. She gained prominence in the global poker community by becoming the first Indian woman to win a World Series of Poker (WSOP) bracelet in 2018, alongside her teammate Giuseppe Pantaleo in the $1,000 No-Limit Hold'em Tag Team event.

With a strong background in finance and marketing, Nikita initially started her journey in the corporate world but soon discovered her passion for poker. She has played at some of the biggest poker tournaments across the world, including the World Poker Tour (WPT), Asian Poker Tour (APT), and European Poker Tour (EPT).

Her fearless and analytical approach to the game has earned her a reputation as one of India's top poker professionals. Beyond her achievements on the felt, Nikita is also an advocate for women in poker and actively works to encourage more female participation in the sport.

My Poker Philosophy

Poker is not just a game of cards; it's a game of people, psychology, and mathematical precision. My approach combines analytical thinking with intuitive reads on opponents. I believe that success in poker comes from continuous learning, adapting to changing dynamics, and maintaining emotional discipline.

As a female player in a predominantly male sport, I've had to overcome additional challenges and stereotypes. This experience has strengthened my resolve and made me a more resilient competitor. I'm passionate about breaking barriers and inspiring the next generation of poker players, especially women, to pursue their dreams in this intellectually stimulating game.

When I'm not at the poker table, I enjoy traveling, reading psychology books, and mentoring aspiring players. I believe in giving back to the community that has given me so much.

My Poker Journey

Explore the key milestones and moments that have shaped my career in professional poker.

2014

Poker Beginnings

Started playing poker professionally after discovering a passion for the game during college. Began competing in local tournaments in Delhi.

2016

First International Tournament

Competed in my first international tournament in Macau, finishing in the money and gaining valuable experience against global competition.

2017

Macau Poker Cup Victory

Won the HK$4,000 NLH Teams Event at the Macau Poker Cup, marking my first major international victory.

2018

WSOP Bracelet Win

Made history by becoming the first Indian woman to win a WSOP bracelet in the $1,000 No-Limit Hold'em Tag Team event with teammate Giuseppe Pantaleo.

2019

WPT500 Las Vegas Runner-Up

Finished as runner-up in the prestigious WPT500 Las Vegas, competing against some of the best players in the world.

2020-2021

Online Poker Success

Adapted to the pandemic by excelling in online poker tournaments, securing multiple final table finishes in major online series.

2022-Present

Global Poker Ambassador

Expanded role as a poker ambassador, representing India on the global stage and advocating for more diversity in the game.
